Banana Bread

The easiest banana bread you will ever make in your life!

  1. Mash bananas until all lumps are gone.
  2. Disolve baking soda in 2 tbsp of hot water.
  3. Add soda to mashed bananas and stir.
  4. Mix all of the raining ingredients to the bananas.
  5. Add chopped walnuts if desired.
  6. Pour mixture into a greased pan of your choice.
  7. Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes or until kinfe inserted in the middle comes out clean.

Ingredients

  • Two medium bananas
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup walnut (optional)
