Chocolat Peanut Butter Overnight Oats

Effortless Breakfast!

Measurements for this recipe depend on size of your container and your preference.

  1. Add oats to container of choice.
  2. Add desired amount of cacao powder into container.
  3. Add chia seeds.
  4. Add peanut butter or nut butter of your choice.
  5. Add maple syrup as a sweetener or agave.
  6. Add your choice of milk.
  7. Mix all together and let it sit over night in the fridge.
  8. Before consumption add topping if desired.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Cacao powder
  • Chia seeds
  • Peanut butter
  • Maple syrup
  • Almond milk
